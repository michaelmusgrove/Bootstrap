About Free Bootstrap and Michael Musgrove

I began using Bootstrap after fumbling around with free and paid WordPress themes such as Thesis (both v. 1 and 2), Elegant Themes, and a bunch of others trying to set up a totally unique experience for the user, without it becoming a lifelong project. I knew right away that Bootstrap was what I was looking for.

With only a little coding knowledge I was setting up sites just like I envisioned in no time, and with tons of functionality, but lightweight and modern.

I noticed there wasn't much detailed documentation for someone getting started with Twitter Bootstrap, so I wrote some. Actually, I wrote a lot. It became a mission to create a comprehensive reference for Bootstrap.

After all, the more people that use and are involved in Bootstrap, the better it, and the resources for it, will become.

And that's how this began. I plan on updating the site and the book often, as new resources appear frequently that are worth using as a resource in your design and development.

If there's something I missed or you think should be included(or removed), let me know!
